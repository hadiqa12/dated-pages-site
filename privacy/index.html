Dated Pages Privacy

Privacy Policy

Effective 25 June 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Dated Pages app for mobile devices, together with any related services operated by Scripting Moss LLC (collectively, the “Application”). Scripting Moss LLC is hereby referred to as the “Service Provider”.

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

The Application does not collect, log, or store any personal information when you download and use it. Registration is not required.

If the Application is used with an active internet connection, technical protocol data (such as your ephemeral IP address) is transmitted to facilitate network connectivity and to retrieve literary content from our database provider. This data is not retained by the Service Provider for tracking or profiling purposes.

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device. The Application reads your device’s local date and time to display the correct calendar day and literary entry for your timezone. This does not involve GPS, location permissions, or transmission of location data.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

Since the Application does not collect personal information through normal use, no user data is shared with third parties by the Service Provider.

The Application connects to third-party infrastructure to deliver content (for example, our database hosting provider). Those providers may process standard network traffic in accordance with their own privacy policies. The Service Provider does not receive personal profiles or identifiable user records from normal use of the Application.

Purchases

Dated Pages is a paid application. Payment is processed entirely by Google Play (for Android) or the Apple App Store (for iOS), as applicable. The Service Provider does not receive or store your payment card details or billing information. Payment processing is governed by Google’s or Apple’s respective terms and privacy policies.

External links

The Application may open external websites in your browser, including a form for submitting literary entry suggestions. When you leave the Application, any information you provide on those third-party sites is governed by those providers’ privacy policies, not this one.

What are my opt-out rights?

Since the Application does not collect personal information through normal use, uninstalling it simply removes the Application from your device.

If you contact the Service Provider directly or voluntarily provide information by other means, you may request deletion of that information by contacting datedpages@gmail.com.

Children

The Application is not intended for children under 16 years of age, or such higher age as required by applicable law. The Service Provider does not knowingly solicit data from children or market to them. Since the Application does not collect personal information through normal use, children’s data is not at risk from use of the Application alone. If you voluntarily provide personal information through other means and are under 16 years of age, your parent or guardian must provide consent on your behalf where permitted by law.

Security

Because the Application does not collect personal data through normal use, the risk of personal data exposure from ordinary app use is minimal. However, no security system is completely secure. The Service Provider implements reasonable safeguards to protect systems and any data it holds.

Data Breach Notification

Since the Application does not collect personal data through normal use, the risk of a data breach affecting your personal data from ordinary app use is minimal. If a breach occurs involving any data you have voluntarily provided, the Service Provider will notify you as required by applicable law.

Changes

The Service Provider may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The Service Provider will notify you of material changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy with an effective date. Where required by law, the Service Provider will seek your consent to material changes before they take effect.

Previous versions of this Privacy Policy will be maintained and made available upon request by contacting the Service Provider at datedpages@gmail.com.

This privacy policy is effective as of 2026-06-25.

Your Consent

If you voluntarily provide information to the Service Provider and processing is based on consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time without affecting processing carried out before withdrawal.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, or have questions about the practices described here, please contact the Service Provider via email at datedpages@gmail.com.
